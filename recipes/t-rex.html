T-Rex

Ingredients

  • Amber with mosquito inside.
  • tiny drill.
  • 1ml syringe.
  • DNA analyser.
  • Frog DNA.
  • Cryochamber.
  • Hatching facility.

Steps

  1. Use the tiny drill to make a channel into the amber.
  2. Carefully use the syringe to extract the contents of the mosquito's stomach.
  3. Analyse the DNA in the syringe to confirm it's the blood of a T-Rex.
  4. Extract the T-Rex DNA.
  5. Combine the frog DNA with the T-Rex DNA.
  6. Use the Cryochamber to incubate the T-Rex embryo.
  7. Transfer the T-Rex embryo to the hatching facility and wait for the T-Rex to hatch.
  8. Once hatched, establish a psychic connection with the T-Rex.
