Triceratops

Ingredients

  • Amber with mosquito inside.
  • tiny drill.
  • 1ml syringe.
  • DNA analyser.
  • Frog DNA.
  • Cryochamber.
  • Hatching facility.

Steps

  1. Use the tiny drill to make a channel into the amber.
  2. Carefully use the syringe to extract the contents of the mosquito's stomach.
  3. Analyse the DNA in the syringe to confirm it's the blood of a Triceratops.
  4. Extract the Triceratops DNA.
  5. Combine the frog DNA with the Triceratops DNA.
  6. Use the Cryochamber to incubate the Triceratops embryo.
  7. Transfer the Triceratops embryo to the hatching facility and wait for the Triceratops to hatch.
  8. Once hatched, establish a psychic connection with the Triceratops.
