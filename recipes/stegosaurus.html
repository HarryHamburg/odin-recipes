Stegosaurus

Ingredients

  • Amber with mosquito inside.
  • tiny drill.
  • 1ml syringe.
  • DNA analyser.
  • Frog DNA.
  • Cryochamber.
  • Hatching facility.

Steps

  1. Use the tiny drill to make a channel into the amber.
  2. Carefully use the syringe to extract the contents of the mosquito's stomach.
  3. Analyse the DNA in the syringe to confirm it's the blood of a Stegosaurus.
  4. Extract the Stegosaurus DNA.
  5. Combine the frog DNA with the Stegosaurus DNA.
  6. Use the Cryochamber to incubate the Stegosaurus embryo.
  7. Transfer the Stegosaurus embryo to the hatching facility and wait for the Stegosaurus to hatch.
  8. Once hatched, establish a psychic connection with the Stegosaurus.
